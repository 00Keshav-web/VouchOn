Gift Cards vs Discount Coupons: Which Saves More in 2025?

Gift cards and coupons both help you save, but which gives better value in 2025? Let’s compare.

Gift Cards

  • ✅ Buy at a discount (₹1000 card at ₹950).
  • ✅ Great for gifting.
  • ❌ Limited to one brand.

Discount Coupons

  • ✅ Instant checkout discounts.
  • ✅ Works on multiple categories.
  • ❌ Short expiry dates.
  • ❌ Restrictions may apply.

Verdict:
- For flexibility & instant discounts → go with coupons.
- For bulk savings & gifting → choose gift cards.
